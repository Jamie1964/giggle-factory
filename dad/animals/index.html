40 Hilarious Animal Dad Jokes

1. What do you call a magic dog?
A Labracadabrador!
2. Why don't elephants use computers?
Because they're afraid of the mouse!
3. What do you call a cold dog?
A chili dog!
4. Why do cows have hooves instead of feet?
Because they lactose!
5. What do you call an alligator in a vest?
An investigator!
6. Why are frogs always so happy?
They eat whatever bugs them!
7. What do you get if you cross a cat with a dark horse?
Kitty Purry!
8. What do you call a sleeping bull?
A bulldozer!
9. Where do sheep go on vacation?

10. Why did the fish blush?
Because it saw the ocean's bottom!
11. What do you call a pig that does karate?

12. What do you call a fly without wings?

13. Why do seagulls fly over the ocean?

16. What do you call a bee that can't make up its mind?

17. What do you call a fish with no eyes?

18. Why did the duck cross the road?
To prove he wasn't chicken!
19. What do you get when you cross a snake and a pie?
A pie-thon!
20. What kind of key opens a banana?
A mon-key!
21. Why are cats bad storytellers?
Because they only have one tale!
22. What do you call a bear with no teeth?

23. Where do polar bears keep their money?

24. What do you call a deer with no eyes?

25. What do you call a deer with no eyes and no legs?

Because it's too far to walk!

30. What do you call a dog magician?

32. Why do bees have sticky hair?
Because they use honeycombs!
33. What do you call an owl that does tricks?

34. How do bees get to school?
On the school buzz!
35. What do you get if you cross a dog and a cheetah?
A dog that runs really fast, but cheats on tests!
36. What do you call a farm animal that never stops talking?
A yak!
37. Why was the caterpillar late?
It took a long time to put on all its shoes!
38. What do you call a snail on a ship?

39. What do you call a penguin in the desert?

40. What is a cat’s favorite color?

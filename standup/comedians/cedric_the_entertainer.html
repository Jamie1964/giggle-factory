Biography

Cedric the Entertainer is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, producer, and television host known for his warm charisma, quick wit, and larger‑than‑life stage presence. Rising to national fame as one of the legendary Original Kings of Comedy, Cedric became a household name through his unforgettable performances, sharp observational humor, and effortless ability to connect with audiences.

His career spans film, television, and stand‑up, with standout roles in Barbershop, The Steve Harvey Show, Johnson Family Vacation, and the CBS sitcom The Neighborhood, where he also serves as an executive producer. Cedric’s comedy blends storytelling, physical humor, and cultural insight, often celebrating family, community, and everyday life with a playful, uplifting style.

With decades of success across multiple entertainment platforms, Cedric the Entertainer remains one of the most beloved and influential figures in modern comedy, continuing to tour, act, and create new projects that showcase his signature charm and comedic craftsmanship.

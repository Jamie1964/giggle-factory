Biography

Brian Regan is an American stand‑up comedian known for his clean, high‑energy comedy, expressive delivery, and universally relatable material. With a career spanning decades, he has become one of the most respected performers in the industry, admired for his ability to make audiences laugh without relying on profanity or shock value.

Regan’s humor blends physicality, sharp observations, and pitch‑perfect timing, making him a favorite among fans of all ages. His acclaimed specials include Brian Regan: Live, Standing Up, and his Netflix projects Nunchucks and Flamethrowers and On the Rocks.

Known for his expressive face, animated storytelling, and clever wordplay, Brian Regan continues to tour extensively and remains one of the most influential and consistently hilarious comedians working today.

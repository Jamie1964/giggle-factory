Biography

Anthony Jeselnik is an American stand‑up comedian celebrated for his dark humor, razor‑sharp timing, and fearless commitment to the joke above all else. Rising from the Los Angeles comedy scene, he gained national attention through late‑night appearances, Comedy Central roasts, and his acclaimed stand‑up specials.

Jeselnik’s comedy is defined by precision, misdirection, and a bold willingness to explore taboo subjects. His Netflix specials, including Thoughts and Prayers and Fire in the Maternity Ward, highlight his mastery of tension and release, earning him a dedicated global audience.

Beyond stand‑up, he hosted The Jeselnik Offensive, wrote for major television shows, and continues to tour internationally. His work stands out for its structure, boldness, and unwavering dedication to comedic craftsmanship.

Biography

Lewis Black is an American stand‑up comedian, actor, author, and social commentator known for his explosive comedic style, trademark rants, and sharp political and cultural satire. His work on The Daily Show and his numerous stand‑up specials have made him one of the most recognizable voices in modern comedy.

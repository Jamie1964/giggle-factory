Biography

Gabriel Iglesias is one of the most successful stand‑up comedians in the world, known for his storytelling, sound effects, and high‑energy performances. Born in San Diego and raised in Long Beach, he built his career through comedy clubs, TV specials, and viral clips that made him a global favorite.

Fun Facts

  • He once performed at Dodger Stadium — the first comedian ever to do so.
  • He collects Volkswagen buses and owns one of the largest collections in the world.
  • He voices characters in Disney, Pixar, and Nickelodeon shows.
  • He worked for a cell phone company before becoming a comedian.

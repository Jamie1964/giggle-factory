Biography

Mo Amer is a Palestinian-American stand‑up comedian, actor, and writer known for his powerful storytelling, sharp cultural commentary, and deeply personal humor. His comedy blends global perspective with intimate reflections on identity, immigration, and family.

Amer gained international recognition through his Netflix specials and his starring role in the acclaimed series Mo, inspired by his own life as a refugee navigating America. His stand‑up is praised for its honesty, warmth, and ability to tackle complex topics with humor and heart.

With a rapidly growing global fanbase, Mo Amer continues to tour, act, and create groundbreaking work that bridges cultures and connects audiences.

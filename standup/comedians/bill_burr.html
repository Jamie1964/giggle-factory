Biography

Bill Burr is an American stand-up comedian, actor, filmmaker, and podcaster. Known for his observational, unfiltered, and relentless comedic style, he is widely regarded as one of the top stand-up comedians of his generation. He created and starred in the animated series F Is for Family and has appeared in hit shows like Breaking Bad and The Mandalorian.
